    Welcome to Xianfeng’s Homepage!!

    Xianfeng Wu (CSIG student member) is currently pursuing a B.E. in Artificial Intelligence at Jianghan University, China, advisor: Associate Researcher Zhongyuan Lai . He is involved in several research projects, including the National Natural Science Foundation of China. He has published 8 papers and held 10 computer software intellectual property rights.

    My current research interests are in AI4Science, Trustworthy Machine Learning and Computer Vision. In particular, I am interested in the applied aspects of 3D Image Processing, Federated Learning, 3D reconstruction and Knowledge Distillation.

    I am currently looking for a 2024fall Ph.D. position, please contact me directly by email me if you think I am match with the research direction of your lab.
